About Us

  • OZAANN Health Care Products are the leading manufacturers of medical disposables, surgical kits and preventive wear made of high-quality materials adhering to international standards.
  • Keeping the health and hygiene of the end user in mind, our products are manufactured in a highly sterile manufactory with utmost care and making the world a better place by reducing the impact on the environment.
  • Our company was established in the year 2000 with a vision to fulfill the demand for cost-effective, prime quality health care consumables that play a pivotal role in deterring the spread of infections and keeping the humanity at large, safe.

  • We take our role of manufacturer and supplier of clinical expendables seriously, since the end users are medically vulnerable individuals who are prone to infections.
  • Our factory is located at Vadapalani, in the heart of Chennai and is highly clean and sanitized.Our employees are experts in the manufacturing process as they are well trained and well experienced, forming the backbone of our company.
  • Over the journey of 20 years in the field, Ozaann has gained a strong foothold in the industry by consistent high-quality products, cost efficient consumables, exceptional customer service, mission to support the health care system efficiently, all the while striving to provide the best products at affordable cost to the consumers.
